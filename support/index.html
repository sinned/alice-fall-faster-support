Support

How can we help?

Playing the game

Drag anywhere to steer Alice. Follow the open path, collect cake or potion power-ups, and use your shield and hearts to stay in the run.

Having trouble?

Try closing and reopening the app. Your best score is saved locally on your device.

Contact

Email sinned@gmail.com with your iPhone model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

We usually reply as soon as possible. Please don’t include passwords or sensitive personal information.
