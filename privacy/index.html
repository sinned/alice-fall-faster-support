Privacy Policy

Your play stays yours.

Effective July 20, 2026

Alice Fall Faster does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Data stored on your device

The app saves your high score locally using Apple’s on-device preferences system. This information stays on your device and can be removed by deleting the app.

No accounts, analytics, ads, or tracking

The app has no account system, analytics service, advertising network, tracking technology, or third-party software development kit. It does not request access to your contacts, photos, location, microphone, camera, or other sensitive device data.

Children’s privacy

Because the app does not collect personal data, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If the app’s data practices change, this policy will be updated before those changes take effect.
